There are a number of digital apps you can use that will help you get your teeth whiter. Some popular apps are listed below.

Teeth Whitening Care

 WEBSITE: https://play.google.com/store/apps/developer?id=Digital+Professional+Apps

ITUNES: N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Teeth.Whitening.Care&hl=en

Function: To provide home remedies, treatments, and solutions for whiter teeth.

Background: Made by Digital Professional Apps, the company behind many other digital apps, too numerous to mention.

General Comments: There are some interesting guides and treatments listed here.

What It Does Best: Delivers information on potential treatments for teeth whitening.

Why It’s Famous: It’s one of a handful of apps devoted to teeth whitening.

The Drawback: Nothing exists for Apple devices.

Teeth Whitening Tips - Learn How to Whiten Teeth

WEBSITE: https://orca.tech

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/no/app/teeth-whitening-tips-learn-how-to-whiten-teeth/id1114712095?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: Filled with info and videos on how to whiten teeth. 

Background: Designed by Nick Lim.

General Comments: A good resource for teeth whitening tips.

What It Does Best: Lists range from whitening teeth in braces to removing yellow between teeth.

Why It’s Famous: Because its lists are pretty comprehensive and it provides videos too.

The Drawback: Nothing exists for Google Play.

Teeth Whitening Tips

WEBSITE: http://www.creativeglance.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/teeth-whitening-tips/id367225211?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.freeappsforyou.classiccarteethwhitening&hl=en

Function: Designed to provide natural ways to brighten teeth. 

Background: Put together by Creative Glance.

General Comments: An interesting app that offers up unconventional thoughts.

What It Does Best: Highlights natural means to whiten teeth.

Why It’s Famous: Great reviews and easy to navigate.

The Drawback: It hasn’t been updated since 2015.

Virtual Teeth Whitening

 

WEBSITE: http://www.clearlytrained.com/app-support/

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/virtual-teeth-whitening/id633567931?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: Play at being a dentist while learning how to whiten your teeth! 

Background: Created by Clearly Trained/Surgery Squad.

General Comments: Take on the role of a Virtual Dentist – You are in control as you perform a teeth whitening procedure on a patient’s teeth in this interactive educational game!

What It Does Best: Gives you a hands-on experience.

Why It’s Famous:  Ranked #1 “Health & Fitness” app in 2 countries.

The Drawback: It’s not the real deal, but it’s great to help kids learn.

Teeth Whitening

WEBSITE: http://appsbay.co.uk

ITUNES: N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.v1_4.B3CD4E28FDA4E17FF8AD05C9.com

Function: Find out all about tooth whitening and cosmetic dentistry on this app.

Background: Made by Venture Technology Limited.

General Comments: You can investigate all forms of teeth whitening methods, side effects and more.

What It Does Best: You can find out where to get Teeth Whitening Kits and how to avoid yellow teeth.

Why It’s Famous: It’s free!

The Drawback: No version for Apple products.

LED Teeth Whitening Mobile App

WEBSITE: https://www.gocanvas.com/mobile-forms-apps/7125-LED-Teeth-Whitening

ITUNES: N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: The LED Teeth Whitening Consent form mobile app offers a convenient way to get patient consent prior to delivering in-office whitening treatments. 

Background: Aimed at dentists looking to track patient consent and signatures, this app handily generates a form the patient can fill out electronically via their smartphone/tablet/device.

General Comments: Patients can pick up information on teeth whitening including risks associated with each method.

What It Does Best: It's the fastest, easiest way to get patient consent before patients undergo professional teeth whitening procedures.

Why It’s Famous: No matter what type of LED teeth whitening systems you're looking for, this app eases and simplifies the journey a patient takes from start to finish.

The Drawback: It’s for dentists only, and there’s nothing to download from the App Store or Google Play.
